Lifeline - Marija Zografska

Early life

Marija Zografska was born on 1st of March in Skopje, Macedonia.She is the only child of Dubravka Zografska, an accountant, and Toni Zografski, a sports journalist. She is of paternal Macedonian and maternal Serbian decent. As a child, she divided her summers between Skopje and Banja Luka,Republika Srpska.

Childhood

Marija grew up as an ordinary child. Her main interests were drawing and visiting the ZOO during weekend. At the age of 6,she was enrolled at Kole Nedelkovski Elementary School. She would finish her primary education there, and discover her passion for mathematics and social sciences. Later on, as a straight A's student she would continue her education at SUGS Orce Nikolov.

Adolescence

She spent her high school mainly socializing with friends and visiting extracurricular activities. Her after school activities consisted of visiting various language courses - English, German and Slovene and going to debate clubs. In year 3, she discovered her passion for informatics and decided to enroll in a technical university. Today, she is a student at the Faculty of Information Technologies and Information Sciences in Skopje.
